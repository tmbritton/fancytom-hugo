I drew this sketch of Barad-dûr because I don't have many architectural sketches in the rotation. They're a good exercise, figuring out what details to keep and what to drop, there's too much in a tower like that to draw every line. You have to choose the ones that sell the shape. Horizon line matters too, which I ignored here, and the top ended up flat.
