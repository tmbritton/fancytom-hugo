I find mechanical objects really hard to draw. You gotta take into account perspective, ellipses, and straight lines. But that's the point of these daily dynamic sketch exercises: draw what's hard, learn the forms, get better. As John F. Kennedy said, "We do not draw these things because they are easy, but because they are hard."
