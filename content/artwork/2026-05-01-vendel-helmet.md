I drew this Swedish Vendel Period helmet this morning. The simpler forms gave me time to get into texture a little bit, trying to get that heavy, beaten-iron look in ink. I saw Amon Amarth last week, watching them channel the Viking aesthetic just made want to draw something built for a shield wall. And I figured I'd take it easy on the perspective after yesterday's attempt at the Millennium Falcon.
