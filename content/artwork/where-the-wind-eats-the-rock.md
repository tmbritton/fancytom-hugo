This is the first automatic drawing I'm posting. The idea is to clear the mind of all intention, move the hand, and just let the drawing flow. Sometimes I'll see something in the drawing and start clarifying, and it becomes something recognizable. Sometimes they're alright, sometimes they're shit. But they're low commitment, so if I like 1 out of 3, it's a win.
