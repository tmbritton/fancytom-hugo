Insects are great practice for depicting form. The segmentation of their exoskeletons is basically built-in contour lines. Their legs also have a nice rhythm of C-curves that's quite nice to draw. I give insects an A+ in being fun to draw. Bumblebees are interesting in particular because they have a combination of fur and segmentation that provides a contrast of textures to draw.
