The la-Tension is the duality of practice. 

## The Workout
Focused on the structured, mechanical grind of expanding the visual library through Dynamic Sketching, anatomy studies, and master copies.

## The Play
Dedicated to automatic drawing and the exploration of the subconscious, plucking ideas from the universal void in the tradition of the surrealists and Austin Osman Spare.

The library is the fuel. As the structured studies grow, the automatic drawings evolve.
